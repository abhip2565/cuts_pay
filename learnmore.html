    Follow These Quick And Simple Steps To Get Started With The CUTS System

    Get Your CUTS Token At Your Nearest Distribution Center

    Small, Lightweight And Loaded With Features! Get Your CUTS Token From The Nearest Distribution Center And Recharge It Online Or Offline At Your Nearest Merchant With A CUTS Box.

    Visit Any CUTS Equipped Merchant And Use Your CUTS Token To Make A Transaction

    It Really Is That Simple! Just Touch And Type In The Amount And Your Passcode And You're Done! No Hassle, No Delay And Most Importantly Never Worry About The Ominous Transaction Failed Popups Ever Again!

    Enjoy Fast, Safe And Secure Transactions With No Worries With Assured Benefits

    We Employ Secure Encryption Models And Use A Completely Offline NFC Bases System To Ensure That You Are Always Protected And Your Transactions Are Safe - With The Added Bonus Of Never Being Connected To The Internet And Thus Never Being Vulnerable To Online Attacks!

    Easily recharge your cuts token from the cuts box itself

    Recharge is done from the CUTS Box, either by placing your card for 30 seconds, and then going for a OTP verification (if you have already paid online) or by directly paying the merchant in cash and getting your card recharged immediately.

    Explore The CUTS Box And What Goes On Under The Hood

    One time installation for merchants

    After a quick KYC, the CUTS box is good to go. It is battery rechargeable, with a lifetime of 5 days just by 3 hours of charge.

    No Extra charges from the merchants to accept payments

    For offline payments CUTS Box acts as a safe, offline, physical wallet for the merchant. Transactions are processed immediately. Balance update takes place immediately. No extra charges for wallet transfer to account. Moreover you can also opt to get physical cash (due to unavailability of bank account).

    Supports both offline and online mode of transaction

    The CUTS box works 24x7, even in situation of complete internet failure. Transaction details are mailed and sent via SMS within neglible delay.

    Easy to use for all ages and for all levels of literacy

    CUTS box is manual POS, with a simple keypad and buttons. Payments, transfers are no more a hassle of handling complex apps.
